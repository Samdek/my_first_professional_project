What I do

Graphic Design

I create visual concepts, using computer software or by hand, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and captivate consumers. I develop the overall layout and production design for applications such as advertisements, brochures, magazines, reports, etc.

Videography

I record live events and small scale video productions. I uses a video camera to record footage for weddings, documentaries, training videos, commercials, TV shows, infomercials, live events, corporate promotions, legal depositions, movies or other films.

Animation

I also produces multiple images called frames, which when sequenced together create an illusion of movement; this is known as animation. It can be made up of digital or hand-drawn pictures, models. I tend to work in 2D, 3D model-making, computer-generated animation.
